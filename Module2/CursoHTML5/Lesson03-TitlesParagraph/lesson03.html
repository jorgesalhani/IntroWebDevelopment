Presenting: Ishtar

Inanna[a] is an ancient Mesopotamian goddess associated with love, beauty, sex, war, justice and political power.
She was originally worshiped in Aratta and Sumer under the name "Inanna", and was later worshipped by the
Akkadians, Babylonians, and Assyrians under the name Ishtar.
